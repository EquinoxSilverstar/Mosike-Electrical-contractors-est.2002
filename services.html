Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our electrical services

Are your electricians licensed and insured?

Yes, all our electricians are fully licensed, certified, and insured. We prioritize safety and compliance with all local and national electrical codes. We also carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage for your peace of mind.

How much does a typical electrical service call cost?

Our service calls typically start at $89 for diagnosis and the first hour of labor. Additional costs depend on the specific requirements of your electrical issue, parts needed, and complexity of the work. We always provide a detailed quote before beginning any work.

Do you offer emergency electrical services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency electrical services for critical situations. Our emergency response team is available day and night to handle urgent electrical issues such as power outages, electrical fires, exposed wiring, and other hazardous conditions.

What warranty do you offer on your services?

We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. Most of our electrical services come with a 1-year warranty on labor and workmanship. Additionally, manufacturer warranties cover any equipment or parts we install, often ranging from 1-10 years depending on the product.

How long does a typical electrical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Minor repairs or installations may take only a few hours, while complete home rewiring or major commercial projects could take several days to weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your project.
