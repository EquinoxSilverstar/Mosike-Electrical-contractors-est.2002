Common Questions

What standards do you follow?

All designs comply with SANS 10142-1 wiring code, IEC standards, and local municipal regulations.

How long does the design process take?

Typical residential projects take 3-5 working days, while commercial projects may require 1-2 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you provide as-built documentation?

Yes, we deliver complete documentation packages including circuit diagrams, load calculations, and equipment specifications.
