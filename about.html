ABOUT US



Mosike Electrical Contractors

From Power Distribution to Innovative Solutions
Born from a commitment to excellence in electrical engineering and contracting, Mosike Electrical Contractors has been dedicated to providing reliable electrical solutions since 2010. Our mission is rooted in safety and quality workmanship, where proper electrical systems form the foundation of every modern structure—just as a building requires a solid foundation to withstand the test of time.


At Mosike, we embrace innovative technologies and industry best practices to "energize" your projects, delivering reliable power systems and electrical installations that meet the highest standards.

Our commitment to excellence is further amplified with our comprehensive electrical services, offering proven solutions to address Commercial, Residential, and Industrial electrical needs.

By ensuring code compliance and promoting energy efficiency, Mosike Electrical Contractors delivers solutions that go beyond basic electrical work—supporting sustainable practices, smart building technology, and overall electrical system health.

Join us on this journey of innovation and let our master electricians transform your electrical systems with confidence and expertise.
