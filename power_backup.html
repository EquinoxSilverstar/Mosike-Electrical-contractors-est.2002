Power Backup FAQs

What size generator do I need?

The required generator size depends on the total wattage of appliances you want to power. Our experts will perform a load calculation to recommend the perfect size.

How long does a UPS last?

UPS runtime varies based on its capacity and the load connected. It's typically designed to provide enough time for a safe shutdown or for a generator to start.

Do you offer maintenance for backup systems?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans to ensure your power backup system is always ready and in optimal condition.
