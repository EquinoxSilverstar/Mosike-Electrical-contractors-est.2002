Wiring FAQs

How often should wiring be inspected?

It's recommended to have your electrical wiring inspected every 5-10 years, or when buying/selling a property, or after major renovations.

What is a Certificate of Compliance (CoC)?

A CoC is a legal document certifying that the electrical installation is safe and complies with SANS 10142-1 regulations. It's required for property sales and new installations.

Can I do my own electrical wiring?

For safety and legal compliance, all significant electrical wiring work must be performed by a registered and qualified electrician who can issue a CoC.
